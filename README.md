# PrintSquare

Prints a square of numbers. The command takes a square number and prints out a pattern, starting with 1 in the middle and spiraling outwards counterclockwise.

## Installation

    $ gem install print_square

## Usage

    $ print_square 16
    16 15 14 13
     5  4  3 12
     6  1  2 11
     7  8  9 10
